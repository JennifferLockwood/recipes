Ingredients
Steel cut oats – 2 cups 
Water – 4 cups 
Milk (any type) – 4 cups 
Butter – 2 tbs. 
Brown sugar – ½  cup 
Kosher salt – 1 tsp. 
Cinnamon – 1 tbs. 
Star anise (optional) – 2 to 3 
1. Optional: Give the slow cooker a quick spray of cooking oil or brush with cooking oil to prevent sticking of ingredients 
2. Put all ingredients in a slow cooker 
3. Cover and cook on low for 8 hours or 4 hours on high 
4. Top with nuts, yogurt, fruit, jams, coconut shavings! 
5. Steel cut oats use a 4:1 liquid to oats ratio. You can use any combination with the liquid – water, soy milk, almond milk, milk all work 
6. If you don’t have a slow cooker, bring the ingredients to boil and then simmer for 45 minutes, stirring here and there to make sure it doesn’t stick to the bottom of the pan
